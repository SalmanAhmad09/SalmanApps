Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Salman Ahmad ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications available on the Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our applications.

By using our apps, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only minimal information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage analytics such as app launch frequency, feature usage patterns, and general interaction data. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Device Information: We may collect general device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Crash Data: If the app crashes, anonymous crash reports may be collected to help us identify and fix issues.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, photos, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information through our apps unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, by contacting us via email for support).

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Maintain and improve the functionality and performance of our apps
  • Identify and fix bugs or technical issues
  • Understand general usage trends to guide future development
  • Respond to your support requests if you contact us directly

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Analytics: We may use Apple's built-in analytics to understand general usage trends. This data is anonymised and aggregated by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

We carefully select any third-party services and only use those that respect user privacy. We do not integrate third-party advertising frameworks.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information through our apps, there is no personal data to delete. If you have contacted us via email, you may request deletion of your correspondence at any time.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information collected. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request information about what data we hold related to you
  • Request deletion of any personal data we may have
  • Opt out of analytics collection (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
